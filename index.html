African Center of Technologies for Inclusive Participation Initiative Limited

Advancing community impact, training, and partnerships for sustainable development.

ACTIP is a registered community based organization that was established in 2023 and officially registered in 2025, dedicated to bridging the digital divide by equipping women, youth and persons with disabilities (PWDs) with inclusive and practical ICT skills. Founded to promote social and economic empowerment through technology, ACTIP implements trainings programs, community outreach, and digital inclusion initiatives across Eastern Uganda and beyond.

Mission Highlights

🌍

Community Empowerment Programs

“Building stronger communities through inclusive innovation.”

ACTIP designs holistic initiatives that address local needs, from digital literacy to sustainable livelihoods. These programs foster resilience, equity, and participation, ensuring that marginalized groups are not left behind in the digital age.

🎓

Skills Training

“Practical skills for real opportunities.”

Hands‑on workshops, mentorship, and digital modules equip youth, women, and persons with disabilities with market‑relevant skills. Training spans ICT, entrepreneurship, and vocational expertise, opening pathways to employment and self‑reliance.

🤝

Community Outreach

“Connecting, listening, and transforming together.”

Through grassroots engagement, ACTIP reaches rural and urban communities with awareness campaigns, mobile clinics, and digital advisory services. Outreach activities amplify voices, share knowledge, and strengthen social bonds.

📢

Advocacy & Partnerships

“Championing inclusion through collaboration.”

ACTIP advocates for policies that promote digital inclusion and partners with governments, NGOs, and private sector actors. These alliances drive systemic change, mobilize resources, and scale impact across regions.

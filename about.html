About Us

Who We Are

ACTIP-African Center of Technologies for Inclusive Participation Initiative Limited is a community-based organization focused on empowering communities, women, youth and PWDs with ICT skills through training, innovation, and advocacy.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower women, youth and PWDs by equipping them with ICT skills that enhance their educational opportunities, enable entrepreneurship, and foster sustainable business growth.

Vision

To create a world where women, youth and PWDs have equal access to digital skills, empowering them to succeed in education, business, and entrepreneurship. We envision a future where technology becomes a catalyst for sustainable development and social inclusion.

Our Values

  • Integrity
  • Inclusivity
  • Innovation
  • Empowerment
  • Collaboration

Impact

What We Do

  • Community training
    At ACTIP our community training programs are designed to equip youths, women and PWDs with practical, future-ready skills. We believe access to knowledge creates opportunity, independence, and sustainable development. Our focus areas include: Digital literacy and AI Training, Basic ICT skilling, Entrepreneurial based skilling.
  • Empowerment programs
    Our empowerment program is designed to unlock the potential of the youths, women and PWDs by equipping them with skills, confidence, and opportunities for sustainable growth. We believe empowerment is not just about training, it is about transforming. Our focus areas include: youth empowerment through, leadership, career mentorship, innovation training. Women empowerment, through financial literacy skills, access to digital tools for growth.
  • Partnerships with local leaders and organizations
    At ACTIP we believe sustainable impact happens through collaboration. By partnering with local leaders, institutions, and organization, we ensure our programs are community driven, relevant, and inclusive. This is only achieved through collaborations with, district and sub county leaders, community elders, youth representatives, to give guidance to help identify real community needs, mobilize participants effectively, and ensure local ownership of programs. We partner with schools and training institutions, government agencies, youth and women groups, private sector organizations.
